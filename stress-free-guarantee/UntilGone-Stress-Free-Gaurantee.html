THE UNTILGONE.COM

STRESS-FREE GUARANTEE

protects you when you purchase items sold and fulfilled by a third-party vendor.

You may be eligible to request a refund under Your Shopping Guarantee if any of the following apply:

WHERE’S MY PRODUCT

You haven’t received a tracking number and 3 business days have passed since your order was placed or your package does not show movement within 2 business days of receiving your tracking number. You may contact our support team, request an update on your order status, request to cancel your order for a full refund.

INCORRECT ORDER

You received an order that is different than expected and have requested a return with the seller that has not been processed within 2 business days excluding holidays observed by the warehouse or vendor.

EFFICIENT RESOLUTION

You returned your item with a trackable shipping method and a refund has not been issued or a replacement sent within 2 business days of receipt.

Note: You have up to 30 days after the delivery date to request a refund under Your Shopping Guarantee.
